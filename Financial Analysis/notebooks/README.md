# EV Financial Health Dashboard
### Tesla vs BYD vs Ford (2022–2024)

## Overview
Comparative financial analysis of three automotive companies during the EV transition period.

## Companies
- **Tesla (TSLA)** — Pure-play EV, high margin pioneer
- **BYD (BYDDY)** — Chinese EV giant, volume-driven
- **Ford (F)** — Legacy automaker navigating EV transition

## Metrics Analyzed
- Net Profit Margin
- Free Cash Flow
- Return on Assets

## Data Limitations
- yfinance free tier limits history to 3 years (2022–2024)
- Fiscal year assumption: all companies use December year-end
